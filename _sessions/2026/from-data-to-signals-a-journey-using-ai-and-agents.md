# From Data to Signals: A Journey Using AI and Agents

**Friday, April 17, 2026 · 1:10 PM – 1:40 PM · Auditorium 2**

---

This presentation will explore how leading enterprises transform vast amounts of data into actionable signals. At S&P Global, our primary focus is delivering valuable insights to our business units and providing exceptional service to our customers. By harnessing raw information from both public and private sources, we have implemented advanced AI technologies and intelligent agents to address complex, multi-modal data challenges. Join us as we guide you through our transformative journey, highlighting real-world use cases that demonstrate the power and impact of these innovative solutions.
