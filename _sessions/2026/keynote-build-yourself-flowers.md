# Keynote: Build Yourself Flowers

**Friday, April 17, 2026 · 4:00 PM – 5:00 PM · Auditorium 5**

---

What do we as ML practitioners have in common with Dutch Golden‑Age flower painter Rachel Ruysch?

This talk is about what all aspects of mastery over millennia have in common, and how we, even in an era of mechanical mass code reproduction, can balance new tools with sound engineering practices to create platforms, projects, tools, and outcomes that satisfy sound engineering practices, speed, and our need for ownership.
